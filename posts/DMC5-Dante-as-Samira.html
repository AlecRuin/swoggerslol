Mod info

For BASE SKIN Samira only. Will not work with other skins!

Description

J A C K P O T.
This is a reskin of Kinghound's Dante as Samira skin The original mod used DMC3 Dante. This mod replaces the voicelines and model with DMC5 assets.

Credits

The assets were sourced by CAPCOM's DMC5 video game! All rights go to their respective owners. I do not claim to own any of the work/assets used by the mod. I do claim ownership of the effort put behind the formation of the mod itself.

Bugs

His body looks horribly deformed. Yes, I know. Samira's body DOES NOT line up to Dante's at all. Because of this, the sword is also piercing his back. To fix this, you'd need to reanimate the skin, and there's around 80 animations for base skin Samira. And I don't get paid to do any of this. My obsession fuels me.

Optional software

I made a macro that scans your screen and plays music based on your ult status as Samira. You can use any song you'd like, but I personally use Subhuman and Devil Trigger. You must have a way to measure your screen, in pixels, and a text editor. If you have any troubles, or want the songs I use, please join my discord server and ping me!
